What Is Recursion? 🪞

Recursion is when a function calls... itself.

🪞 Mirror Example:

A mirror facing another mirror — You see yourself inside yourself… again and again.

📦 Box Example:

Open a box, and inside is another box. And another. And another. You stop when you reach the last one.

That last box is called the base case.

🔁 Why Recursion Is Powerful:

  • Solves complex problems step by step
  • Useful for trees, searching, and algorithms
  • Elegant way to repeat with purpose
Recursion isn’t magic — just repetition with a smart ending.
