Loops in Programming 👋

Why write the same thing 5 times, when a loop can do it for you?

🔁 Real Life Example:

Imagine you want to say “hi” to 5 people.

Hi  
Hi  
Hi  
Hi  
Hi

OR just say:

“Repeat ‘hi’ 5 times.”

That’s a loop.

🛠️ Types of Loops:

  • for loop → Do this X times
  • while loop → Keep doing this until I say stop

🔓 Why Loops Matter:

  • Reduce code repetition
  • Automate boring tasks
  • Save you from copying & pasting
If you're repeating something — there's probably a loop for it.
